About Our School

Our Story

Founded in 1990, Francolin Primary School has been serving the community with quality primary education for over 30 years. We started as a small neighborhood school and have grown into a respected institution known for our child-friendly approach to learning.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every child is unique and deserves an education that nurtures their individual talents while providing a strong foundation in core academic skills.
